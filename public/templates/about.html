Who Is ALPHA?

Cultivators of Elite Athletes

Justin "Jay" Haynes

Owner/Head Coach

Certifications:

USA Weightlifting Level II

CrossFit Level I

CPR

Coach Jay heads the ALPHA Olympic Weightlifting programming and oversees all operations under the ALPHA umbrella. Born and raised in the Bay Area of Northern California, Justin grew up with a love of sports participating in baseball, basketball, football and track & field. Coach Jay graduated from Armijo HS (Fairfield, CA) where he excelled in both football and track & field. This is where his kinesthetic awareness began and he honed his athletic skills. Coach Jay has always had a passion for coaching youth athletes as he has in baseball and youth football. With a passion for the sport of weightlifting and sports performance, in 2013 he made the decision to continue his own coaching development obtaining his CrossFit Level I and USA Weightlifting I and II Coaching Certifications. He is an active competitor in Olympic Weightlifting and continues to develop his experience and skill-sets, focusing on leading by example. Coach Jay believes that building a proper foundation for an individual/athlete is what has a vital and long lasting impact. Coach Jay uses his own personal and professional experiences to give back to the community that has served him well.

Blaire "B" Camarda

Director of Fitness and Sports Performance

Certifications:

Certified Personal Trainer, CPT

Certified Sports Conditioning Specialist, CSCS

USA Track and Field, Certified Coach

CPR

Coach Blaire oversees individual and group fitness, as well as sports performance training for ALPHA. Born and raised in San Diego County, she grew up participating in a variety of sports including basketball, horse back riding, tennis and softball. She played basketball at Carlsbad HS (Carlsbad, CA) where she was a 4 year Varsity starter and participated in collegiate basketball at Concordia University (Irvine, CA) and Chapman University (Orange, CA). After college, she coached varsity basketball for 6 years in Orange County, CA where she started her personal training and sports performance coaching. While in Orange County, she also coached Co-ed varsity Track & Field, Softball and Co-ed Cross Country. Coach B most recently served as an Assistant Coach and Strength and Conditioning Coach for Sage Creek HS Boys Basketball (Carlsbad, CA). She is a Certified Personal Trainer (CPT), Certified Sports Conditioning Specialist (CSCS) and USA Track and Field Certified Coach (USATF). She is looking forward to developing athletes in Solano and Napa counties and making an impact with the ALPHA family.

Pieter "Piete" Lentz, Jr.

Head Strength Coach

Certifications:

USA Powerlifting Level I

Coach Piete contributes to the Strength and Powerlifting programming as well as oversees all Powerlifting classes. Coach Piete is born and raised in the Bay Area (Suisun City, CA). He graduated from Armijo HS (Fairfield, CA) where he excelled in football and weightlifting. After high school, Pieter served 4 years in the army where he served multiple deployments overseas. During this time, Coach Piete strengthened his passion for Powerlifting. Upon his return from active duty he has grown his experience, knowledge and skills in the sport of Powerlifting. He has stayed consistently active in the Powerlifting community, is a current competitor in USAP events and can't wait to flex his guns with the ALPHA family.
